Proper Aspiration Technique Immersion depth: Tip 2-3mm below liquid surface (avoid capillary action) Angle: Hold pipette vertically or max 20° tilt Button control: Press to first stop → immerse → slowly release to aspirate 2. Pre-wetting (Critical for Accuracy) Why: Saturate tip air space, equilibrate temperatures How: Aspirate/dispense 2-3 times before actual measurement Result: Prevents evaporation loss in first dispense 3. Dispensing Technique Angle: 20-45° against vessel wall First stop: Dispense main volume Second stop (blow-out): Empty tip completely Touch-off: Wipe droplet on vessel wall 4. Pipette Positioning Vertical aspiration (±20°) = most accurate Tilted dispensing = smooth flow Consistent angle = reproducible results
