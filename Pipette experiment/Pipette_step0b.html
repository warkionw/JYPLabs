🎥 HOW TO USE A PIPETTE

🧪 Theory

Proper Aspiration Technique

• Immerse Tip 2-3mm below liquid surface to avoid air bubbles.
• Hold pipette vertically or max 20° tilt
• Press to first stop → immerse → slowly release to aspirate

Pre-wet

• Pre-wetting the pipette tip with the solution helps to improve accuracy.
• Why is this important? It reduces the chances of air bubbles and ensures that the liquid adheres properly to the tip, leading to more consistent and accurate measurements.
• Aspirate/dispense 2-3 times before actual measurement. (Basically pipetting the liquid up and down)

Dispensing Technique

• Dispense at angle 20-45° against vessel wall.
• Depressing plunger to First stop is to dispense the main volume.
• While depressing to Second stop ensures complete dispensing by expelling any residual liquid in the tip.
• After dispensing, keep the tip in contact with the vessel wall for a moment to allow liquid to flow out completely.

Pipette Position

• Maintain a vertical position while aspiration (±20°) to ensure accuracy.
• Tilt pipette slightly (±20°) during dispensing to allow liquid to flow smoothly and prevent splashing.
