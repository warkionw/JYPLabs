About

CCEB Student Platform is designed for bioengineering students in Singapore to gain hands-on experience with lab techniques through interactive virtual simulations. Our platform provides comprehensive learning modules covering essential experimental procedures including pipetting, gel electrophoresis, HPLC analysis, and more.

Developed by the Centre for Chemical and Environmental Biology (CCEB), this platform aims to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical skills, enabling students to learn at their own pace with engaging, interactive content.
