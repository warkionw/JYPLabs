About This Virtual Module

• Protein will separate from each other while they migrate through the pH gradient due to the voltage.
• Proteins will stop migrating when they reach the pH that matches their isoelectric point (pI). This is also known as the neutral net charge point.
• Hence at the end of the electrophoresis, the protein will be separated based on their isoelectric points.
• This method can separate protein up to a difference of 0.1 pH units.
• Proteins will form bands on the gel which can be used for further experimentation.

Experiment Procedures

• IEF is usually followed by 2D gel electrophoresis, also known as SDS-PAGE
• It further allows the separation of protein.
• It is widely used to find the presence of protein, expression protein or the identification in different cells or tissue.
