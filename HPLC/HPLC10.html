🧪 Analysis

Analysis

• Detector signal intensity: Measured in mAu (milli-Absorbance Units). It indicates the concentration of analyte present and the absorption of the samples.
• Time: This refer to the retention time. Which is the time between the injection and detection of the analyte. It allows for the identification of the specific components within the mixture.
• Peak area: The area under the curve of the peak. It is directly proportional to the concentration of the analyte in the sample. It is used for quantification purposes.

Even though everything can be calculated through software, however looking at the chromatography allows one to check the accuracy of the analysis.
